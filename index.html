Empowering Kids Through Tech Education

Creating learning experiences that go beyond just programming.

Our Mission

Tech4Kids was created with a dream of helping kids learn tech from a young age. We believe it all starts with education. Our interactive, hands-on workshops take students beyond the screen, diving deep into hardware construction, electronic circuit configurations, and basic digital safety.

What We Teach & Do

Hands-on Building

Students get their hands dirty assembling real PCs from scratch, learning how CPUs, RAM, and internal storage units cooperate.

Soldering & Electronics

Introducing foundational hardware engineering concepts and circuit construction using completely safe, guided soldering safety profiles.

Basic Cybersecurity

Teaching vital digital hygiene, secure file sharing habits, network structures, and safe online navigation rules from day one.

Tech Recycling

Putting old tech to use! We gather discarded laptops, tablets, and phones to serve as direct interactive instruments in our physical learning labs.
