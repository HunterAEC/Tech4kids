Empowering the Next Generation of Technologists

Tech4Kids was created with a dream of helping kids learn tech from a young age, creating learning experiences that go far beyond just standard programming.

Our Core Focus Areas

🛠️ Hardware Assembly

We provide safe, hands-on environments where children learn to assemble actual physical computers, understand circuits, and recognize essential motherboard processors.

🔥 Engineering & Soldering

Moving past standard screens, our labs guide senior students through basic soldering safety, custom component routing, and minor engineering concepts.

🛡️ Basic Cybersecurity

We believe it all starts with secure digital education. Young minds practice navigating networks, setting strong security parameters, and surfing safely.

Put Your Old Technology To Better Use

The hardware you don't need can help a child learn! Tech4Kids wants to salvage your old tech and turn it into real-world educational components. We are actively collecting computers, tablets, and mobile phones to fuel our classrooms.
