Our Mission

Tech4Kids was created with a dream of helping kids learn tech from a young age. We believe it all starts with education. Our hands-on experiences take students beyond the screen, diving deep into hardware, circuits, and online safety.

What We Teach & Do

Hands-on Building

Students get their hands dirty assembling real PCs from scratch, learning how CPUs, RAM, and storage cooperate.

Soldering & Electronics

Introducing fundamental hardware engineering and circuit construction using safe, guided soldering techniques.

Basic Cybersecurity

Teaching vital digital hygiene, secure coding habits, and safe network navigation principles from day one.

Tech Recycling

Putting old tech to use! We collect discarded computers, tablets, and phones to serve as active learning instruments.
