Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers regarding equipment specifications, safety protocols in our soldering labs, and device data clearing measures.

❓ Do you safely erase my personal files before reusing devices?

Absolutely. Every donated laptop, tablet, and phone goes through a secure formatting and data-destruction protocol. We erase all storage parameters and partition structures completely before students interact with the physical modules.

❓ What age brackets are permitted in the engineering and soldering clinics?

Our core hardware configuration and block programming paths support children ages 8 to 15. Advanced clinics involving circuit building and soldering require senior participant status (ages 12+) along with strict parental verification parameters.

❓ Is there a pickup service available for corporate hardware items?

Yes! For local corporate partners or tech companies donating surplus technology bundles (10 or more devices), our logistics team can coordinate a scheduled on-site package collection. Please use our Contact page to set your layout requirements.

❓ Can my child join a learning lab if they have zero prior tech experience?

Definitely. Tech4Kids is purpose-built to cater to absolute beginners. We design our visual syntax, puzzle-based parameters, and hardware breakdown labs to scale sequentially from the ground up.
