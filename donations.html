Donate Your Electronics

Tech4Kids wants to put your old tech to use! The tech you don’t need can help a child learn. We are always looking for old computers, tablets, and phones to spark hands-on curiosity in our workshops.

Acceptable Donation Priority Guide

Review our needed item specifications below. Devices in working order directly power classroom software and cybersecurity programs, while non-working hardware is highly valued for hardware extraction and building practice.

Device Category Operational Status Priority Tier Classroom Use Case
Old Laptops Working Order ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Top Priority Primary devices used by kids for programming, hardware interfacing, and basic cybersecurity training labs.
Old Tablets Working Order ⭐⭐⭐⭐ High Priority Utilized for responsive web mobile layout testing, visual block coding setups, and interactive digital educational modules.
Old Phones Working Order ⭐⭐⭐⭐ High Priority Used by advanced groups for testing student-made apps, examining configurations, and verifying device deployment.
Old Laptops Non-Working / Broken ⭐⭐ Medium Priority Perfect for computer assembly workshops, allowing kids to learn PC construction by mapping components like CPUs, RAM, and drives.
Old Tablets / Phones Non-Working / Broken ⭐ Low Priority Used safely in hardware breakdown labs to teach physical components, parts harvesting, and entry-level soldering clinics.
