Hardware Technology Donations

Give your retired hardware systems a meaningful second life. We transform surplus equipment into high-impact diagnostic labs for students.

Accepted Equipment Guidelines

To guarantee safety configurations inside our teaching modules, please verify that your items match the hardware performance parameters detailed below:

Device Category Minimum Operational Requirements Status Condition Allowed
Laptops / Desktops Intel Core i3 or equivalent, 4GB RAM minimum capacity Working condition; minor cosmetics acceptable
Tablets & Mobile iOS 12+ or Android 9.0+ processing architectures Functional screens; battery holds basic charge stability
Components & Parts Solid State Drives (SSD), DDR4 memory channels, basic power units Unused or fully tested working modules
Peripherals USB Keyboards, optical mice, functional 1080p screen displays Clean layouts; cable components undamaged
