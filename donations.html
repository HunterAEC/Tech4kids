Donate Your Electronics

The tech you don't need can help a child learn. We are always looking for old computers, tablets, and phones to distribute and tear down in classrooms.

Acceptable Donation & Item Guide

Device Type Target Requirements Classroom Use Case
Desktop / Laptop PCs Intel Core i3 or equivalent, 4GB RAM minimum Software training and complete assembly practice
Tablets Working display screen, power adapter included Mobile application layout labs and digital reading
Smartphones Unformatted, unlocked carriers preferred Mobile hardware deconstruction clinics
