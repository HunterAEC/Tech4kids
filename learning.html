Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our technology learning programs, hardware requirements, or how to get involved? Find quick answers below.

What age group are the Tech4Kids modules designed for?

Our programs are structured primarily for students aged 8 to 15, with entry-level tracks for block programming and advanced paths for physical hardware builds.

Are there any costs or fees associated with the training paths?

No, all our online learning interactive resources, laboratory configurations, and video classes are completely free to public students worldwide.

How can I track student lab completion checkpoints?

Each video resource includes timestamp guides so students can systematically pause, evaluate their configuration milestones, and debug physical parts alongside our instructions.
